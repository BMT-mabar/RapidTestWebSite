Technology in Action

Our computer vision API, running on Edge-AI, delivers rapid and accurate results directly from a smartphone.

Advanced Computer Vision Core

Our API leverages sophisticated algorithms to interpret a wide array of lateral-flow tests with high accuracy, assessing color intensity and delivering a result in under 60 seconds. Built-in QC checks ensure reliable readings every time.

The Edge-AI Advantage

By processing data directly on the user's device, we enhance data privacy and deliver rapid results. This approach eliminates cloud latency and functions effectively even in low-connectivity environments.

Assay-Agnostic & Rapid Onboarding

Our platform is designed to be compatible with various test formats including cassettes, strips, and other devices. A single, robust codebase allows us to efficiently onboard new assays in less than 4 weeks, ensuring adaptability and scalability.
