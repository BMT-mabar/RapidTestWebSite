Our Story: Bridging a Critical Gap in Healthcare

RapidTest.AI was founded to address a critical challenge in the rapidly expanding world of self-testing. While the convenience of at-home diagnostics is undeniable, the valuable data generated often remains isolated, failing to reach clinicians or contribute to broader public health insights. This leads to inefficiencies, missed diagnostic opportunities, and increased healthcare costs.

Our mission is to empower individuals and healthcare providers by transforming the smartphone in your pocket into a sophisticated diagnostic tool. Through our cutting-edge computer vision API, we make it possible to obtain clinical-grade results from standard lateral-flow tests in seconds, ensuring data is accurately captured, securely transmitted, and readily available to those who need it most.
